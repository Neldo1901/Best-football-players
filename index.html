Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese footballer, often considered one of the greatest of all time. Born on February 5, 1985, he started at Sporting CP before making a name at Manchester United. He then moved to Real Madrid, where he became the club’s all-time top scorer and won several Champions League titles. After a stint at Juventus, he returned to Manchester United before joining Al Nassr in 2023. Renowned for his goal-scoring ability and athleticism, he has won multiple Ballon d'Or awards and remains a global sports icon.

Leo Messi

Born in Rosario, Argentina, in 1987, Lionel Messi is widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time, and his illustrious career proves why. The Argentinean footballer, who holds a record eight Ballon d'Or awards, has displayed his talent early on and has always been destined for greatness.

Neymar Jr.

Neymar Jr. is a Brazilian footballer born on February 5, 1992. He started his career at Santos FC, gaining fame for his skill and scoring. In 2013, he joined Barcelona, where he formed a powerful trio with Lionel Messi and Luis Suárez, winning several titles, including the Champions League.

Kylian Mbappe

Kylian Mbappé is a French footballer born on December 20, 1998, in Bondy, France. Known for his speed and scoring ability, he started at AS Monaco before moving to Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) in 2017. At PSG, he has won multiple titles and consistently ranks among the top scorers. Mbappé is also a key player for the French national team, playing a vital role in their 2018 World Cup victory, where he became one of the youngest goal-scorers in a final. He’s widely regarded as one of the top talents in football today.
